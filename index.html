About Unfettered

Unfettered is a 2D platform RPG game with Chinese Xianxia background. It tells a story about doom-background setting with only few human cities survived under the spread of mind-demons.

Features:

- Drawing Fu(patterns) to release skill

- Multi-choice, Multi-end

- Pure Hand Drawing Art

Core Mechanic

Player can slow down the time and drawing some pattern named Fu to release powerful skills. Fu is a Chinese Taoist concept. They believe they can use the power of deity by drawing complicated pattern on a yellow paper.
